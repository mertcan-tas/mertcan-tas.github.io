## Overview

White Rabbit is a Chrome extension that turns your new-tab page into a clean, customizable home base. It pairs a nice background with quick-access shortcuts to your favourite sites, organized into rows you control — so opening a new tab feels useful rather than empty.

## Features

- **Backgrounds** — choose from ready-made options or set your own custom image by URL.
- **Quick-access shortcuts**, organized into customizable rows.
- **Add & manage shortcuts** easily, with a confirmation step for deletes.
- **Fast icon loading** via favicon caching.
- **Settings page** — toggle the background layer, "open in new tab" behaviour, and icon caching.

## Stack

- **Vue 3 + Vuetify + Pinia**, bundled with **Vite**, with **vue-i18n** for translations.
- Packaged as a Chrome extension (Manifest).
